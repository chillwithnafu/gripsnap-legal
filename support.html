Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my high score?

You can reset your game data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app, or by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Why am I seeing ads?

Gridsnap is a free game supported by advertisements. Ads help us keep the game free for everyone.

The game isn't loading properly. What should I do?

Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the issue persists, try restarting your device or reinstalling the app.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us with a description of the issue, your device model, and OS version. Screenshots are helpful!
